NeoHaskell is a Haskell dialect, not a Haskell skin. IO becomes Task with typed errors. Either becomes Result with Ok/Err. Qualified imports are mandatory. Point-free style is forbidden. These aren't bugs — they're deliberate choices. This page explains every difference and why it exists.
